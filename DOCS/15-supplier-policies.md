# GlobexSky — Supplier Policies

## 1. Account Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| One account per business | Only one supplier account per registered business entity |
| Accurate information | All profile, product, and pricing information must be truthful |
| Response time | Must respond to buyer inquiries within 48 hours |
| Ship on time | Must ship within the stated processing time |
| Accurate listings | Product descriptions, images, and specifications must match actual goods |
| Prohibited products | No counterfeit, illegal, or restricted goods |
| Photo authenticity | No stock photos passed off as actual product photos |

---

## 2. Supplier Rating System

### Rating Scale

| Stars | Label | Definition |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| 5 ⭐ | Excellent | Exceeded expectations |
| 4 ⭐ | Good | Met expectations |
| 3 ⭐ | Average | Some issues but acceptable |
| 2 ⭐ | Poor | Did not meet expectations |
| 1 ⭐ | Very Poor | Significantly failed |

### Rating Calculation

- Ratings are **weighted by recency**: recent orders count more than old orders.
- Weight formula: `weight = 1 / (days_since_order / 30 + 1)`
- Overall rating = weighted average of all reviews received.

### Standing Thresholds

| Rating | Status | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| ≥ 4.0 | Good standing | No action required |
| 3.5 – 3.9 | Watch | Monitor, suggestions sent |
| 3.0 – 3.4 | Warning | Formal warning issued |
| 2.5 – 2.9 | Review | Account under review |
| < 2.5 | Risk of suspension | Suspension initiated |

## 4. Dispute Resolution Process

### Dispute Types
- Product not as described
- Product not received
- Damaged product
- Wrong item shipped
- Quantity short
- Quality below stated specification

### Dispute Timeline

```
Day 0:  Buyer opens dispute (within 15 days of delivery)
        │
Day 3:  Supplier must respond (evidence upload encouraged)
        │
Day 6:  If no resolution → GlobexSky mediation begins
        │
Day 11: GlobexSky issues decision
        │
Day 14: Decision executed (refund/partial/replace/reject)
```

### Possible Decisions

| Decision | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| Full refund | 100% buyer refund, order reversed |
| Partial refund | Agreed partial refund for partial fault |
| Replace | Supplier ships replacement (cost to supplier) |
| Reject | Dispute rejected, buyer's claim not upheld |

### Evidence Guidelines

Suppliers should provide:
- Original order confirmation
- Shipping tracking evidence
- Photos of packaged product before shipping
- Communication history with buyer

## 7. Account Violation & Termination

### Violation Process

```
Violation detected (fraud, fake products, policy breach)
        │
Formal warning issued (email + in-app)
        │
(If repeat/serious) → Probation: listing limited, payouts held
        │
(If no improvement) → Account suspended: products hidden, orders blocked
        │
(Final) → Account terminated: permanent ban
```

### Appeal Process

- Supplier has **14 days** to appeal any suspension or termination.
- Appeal submitted via admin support ticket.
- GlobexSky reviews within 5 business days.
- Decision is final after appeal review.

### Post-Termination

- **90-day payout hold**: Remaining earnings held for 90 days for chargeback protection.
- After 90 days, net earnings (minus any chargebacks) released.
- Account and product data retained for 2 years for legal compliance.
- Terminated supplier may not create new accounts.

---

## 8. Intellectual Property Policy

- Suppliers must own or have rights to all product images, descriptions, and trademarks.
- Counterfeit or trademark-infringing listings will be removed without notice.
- Repeat IP violations lead to permanent termination.
- GlobexSky responds to DMCA takedown requests within 48 hours.
